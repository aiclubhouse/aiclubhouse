# Twitter Thread: Best AI Tools for Small Business (Australia)
**Date:** Schedule for Week 2 Saturday (Apr 5)

## Thread (Tweet 1/10)
If you're an Aussie small business owner, you don't need an army of AI tools.

You need 2-3 that actually save time & make money.

After testing 50+ tools, here are my top picks for 2026:

## Tweet 2/10
1. **Semrush** – AI-powered SEO & content

Why it's worth $120/month:
- Cuts keyword research from 3 hours → 30 mins
- AI suggests topics that rank in Australia
- Competitor analysis reveals local opportunities

Best for: businesses that want more Google traffic.

## Tweet 3/10
2. **Jasper** – AI copywriting that gets Aussie English

Why it's worth $49/month:
- Writes in Australian spelling & idioms
- Learns your brand voice over time
- Saves 10+ hours/week on content

Best for: anyone creating blogs, ads, or social content.

## Tweet 4/10
3. **Canva** – AI design for non-designers

Why it's worth $18/month:
- Background removal saves $50-100/design
- Magic Write generates decent captions
- AI image generator for placeholder images

Best for: businesses creating visual content.

## Tweet 5/10
4. **Writesonic** – budget alternative to Jasper

$19/month for 100,000 words.
Great for long-form content.
Australian English support.

Best for: bootstrappers who need volume.

## Tweet 6/10
5. **ChatGPT Plus** – the Swiss Army knife

$25/month.
Best for brainstorming, not final content.
Combine with human editing.

Best for: ideas, outlines, and quick answers.

## Tweet 7/10
My recommended stack for most Aussie SMBs:

Month 1-3: Canva ($18) + ChatGPT Plus ($25) = $43
Month 4+: Add Jasper ($49) when content is a bottleneck
When ready: Add Semrush ($120) for SEO

Total: ~$190/month vs $12,000/month in human labor.

## Tweet 8/10
What to avoid:

- Enterprise AI suites (overkill for SMBs)
- Custom AI development (buy, don't build)
- "Full automation" promises (AI amplifies humans, doesn't replace them)

## Tweet 9/10
Start with ONE tool that solves your biggest pain point:

Need design help? → Canva
Struggle with content? → Jasper
Want more website traffic? → Semrush

Master it, then add another.
