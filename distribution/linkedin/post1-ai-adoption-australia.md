I've been testing AI tools for Australian businesses for the past 6 months.

Here's what I found actually works (and what's overhyped):

✅ **What Works:**

1. **Canva's AI design tools**
   - Background removal saves $50-100/design
   - Magic Write generates decent social captions
   - ROI: 5x for any business creating visual content

2. **Jasper for Australian copywriting**
   - Understands local spelling & idioms
   - Saves 10+ hours/week on content
   - Trains on YOUR brand voice

3. **Semrush for SEO**
   - AI suggests content topics that rank in Australia
   - Competitor analysis reveals local opportunities
   - Pays for itself at 2+ clients/month

4. **ChatGPT/Claude for brainstorming**
   - Free tiers are surprisingly capable
   - Best for ideas, not final output
   - Combine with human editing

❌ **What's Overhyped:**

1. **"Full automation" promises**
   - AI won't replace your marketing team
   - It amplifies good marketers, replaces bad processes

2. **Enterprise AI suites**
   - Most Australian SMBs need 2-3 tools, not 20
   - Start small, scale based on results

3. **Custom AI development**
   - Unless you're an ASX200 company, buy don't build
   - Off-the-shelf AI tools are 90% as good for 10% of the cost

📊 **The Numbers:**

Before AI tools: Working 60-hour weeks, outsourcing content/design
After AI tools: Working 40-hour weeks, higher quality output

💰 **ROI Calculation:**

Canva ($18/month) + Jasper ($49/month) + Semrush ($120/month) = $187/month

Vs. hiring:
- Junior designer: $4,000/month
- Content writer: $3,000/month

Total: $12,000/month vs $187/month

🤔 **The Reality:**

AI won't replace your team. But the Australian business that uses AI WILL replace the one that doesn't.

Start with one tool that solves your biggest pain point:
- Need design help? → Canva
- Struggle with content? → Jasper
- Want more website traffic? → Semrush

Master it. Then add another.
