About AI Clubhouse

Where AI Meets Community 🇦🇺

Our Mission

AI Clubhouse exists to make AI accessible, understandable, and useful for everyday Australians — whether you're a solo entrepreneur, a small business owner, a marketer, a creator, or just curious about what AI can do for you.

We're not here to hype. We're here to help.

What We Do

We test AI tools so you don't have to waste time and money on the wrong ones. Every review on AI Clubhouse is based on hands-on testing with real projects — not press releases, not feature lists, not paid reviews.

  • Honest Reviews: We pay for every tool we review. No vendor-funded reviews. If a tool is mediocre, we'll say so.
  • Practical Tutorials: Step-by-step guides you can follow today. No theory-heavy lectures. Just "here's how to do the thing."
  • Head-to-Head Comparisons: We run tools through identical tests and show you the results. The data picks the winner, not our opinions.
  • Australian Focus: Reviews account for AU pricing (including exchange rates), local payment support, data residency, and use cases relevant to Australian businesses.

How We Make Money

Transparency matters to us. Here's exactly how AI Clubhouse generates revenue:

  • Affiliate commissions: When you click a link to a tool we recommend and sign up, we may earn a commission. This never costs you extra — in many cases, our links include discounts or extended trials.
  • Sponsorships (coming soon): We'll accept sponsored content from AI companies, but it will always be clearly labelled and separate from our editorial reviews.

What we don't do: We never let affiliate relationships influence our reviews. If a tool with a great affiliate program is mediocre, we say so. If a tool with no affiliate program is excellent, we still recommend it. Trust is worth more than commissions.

Full details in our Affiliate Disclosure.

The Community

AI Clubhouse is more than a review site. We're building a community of Australians who are learning, experimenting, and building with AI. Through our Discord, weekly newsletter, and upcoming events, we connect people who share a curiosity about what's possible with artificial intelligence.

Whether you're an AI expert or just getting started, you're welcome here.
