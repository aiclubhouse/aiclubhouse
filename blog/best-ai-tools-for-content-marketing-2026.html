7–10: Honourable Mentions

7. Surfer SEO — Best for Content Optimization

Surfer analyses the top-ranking pages for any keyword and gives you a detailed content brief. Combined with Jasper, it's a lethal SEO combo. Starts at $89/mo.

8. Descript — Best for Podcast & Video Editing

Edit video by editing text. Seriously. It transcribes your content and lets you cut, rearrange, and fix mistakes by editing the transcript. Magic. Starts at $24/mo.

9. Canva AI — Best for Design Content

Magic Write, Magic Design, and background removal make Canva essential for visual content. The AI features are included in the Pro plan ($15/mo).

10. Notion AI — Best for Content Planning

If you plan content in Notion (and you should), the AI add-on drafts briefs, generates outlines, and summarises research. $10/mo add-on to any Notion plan.

Our Verdict

For most content marketers, the winning stack is:

  • Jasper for content creation (or Writesonic if budget-conscious)
  • Semrush for SEO strategy and optimization
  • GetResponse for email marketing and lead nurturing
  • Synthesia for video content

That combination covers writing, SEO, email, and video — the four pillars of modern content marketing. Total cost: roughly $230-350/mo, which pays for itself with one decent client or a few affiliate commissions.

Start with one tool, master it, then expand. Don't try to adopt everything at once.
