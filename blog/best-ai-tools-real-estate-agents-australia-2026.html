You can input the property features (4 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, 2-car garage, pool, quiet cul-de-sac) and Jasper will craft a compelling, emotive narrative in seconds.

3. AI Chatbots for 24/7 Lead Capture

Prospective buyers and sellers don't just browse Property.com.au or Domain during business hours. Most traffic happens at night or on weekends when you're busy at auctions. AI chatbots have evolved beyond simple "if/then" logic; in 2026, they use Natural Language Processing to have real conversations.

Tools like Intercom or specialized real estate bots can now:

  • Qualify leads by asking about their budget, timeline, and property preferences.
  • Book property appraisals directly into your calendar.
  • Answer common questions about local school zones, council rates, and transport links using data from your suburb profiles.
  • Hand off "hot" leads to your phone via SMS the moment someone indicates they are ready to list their home.
